Australian Shepherds

The Californian cowboy dogs trying to pass as Australian.

  1. What are they like?
    • Highly trainable
    • High energy
    • Nice medium size
    • Loyal and loving
    • Long-living
    • Intelligent
  2. Things to consider:
    • Mental stimulation: they need lots of stimulation--if you don't provide it they will find it in ways you won't be happy about.
    • Physical Activity: they need plenty of exercise (this counts as stimulation).
    • Herding: they will try to herd you and constantly keep track of everybody's location.
    • Grooming: they need regular brushing to minimize shedding and get their undercoat off
    • Sneaky: they will use their intelligence to outsmart you. Never leave your food unattended. Don't give in to their manipulation.
    • Wiggle Butt: when they're excited to see you, they will wiggle their butt and circle around you. They will also purr.
  3. Who should get an Australian Shepherd?
    • They are perfect for someone who wants a dog they can take on daily walks or runs and is willing to put in the time and effort to train them--someone active.
    • An even better match is someone who is looking to herd sheep.
